What is a domain?

The domain is the address of your website on the Internet and this address is in the form of an IP / username, but certainly this address will be difficult to remember by Internet users, so you need an easy-to-remember name, which prompts you to reserve a domain so that it is easy for customers to remember your site and enter it at any time The domain works by linking the client and the server. When you connect the domain to the server, where the domain recognizes the server on which your site is located, and the internal DNS settings on the server, you will know your location among all the sites on the server, Thus, it will make it easier for the customer to access your website. The domain will also make it easier for you to transfer your site from any server to another. Where if you rely on the IP for visits, and change the server, the IP will change, and therefore in this case no visitor will be able to enter your site, but with the domain, you can keep the same domain and link to a different server without the client noticing any change.
